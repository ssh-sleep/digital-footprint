## Overview

Gallery of Davao was an independent art gallery I co-founded in 2009 in Davao City.
We existed to give local artists a venue and an audience — through curated exhibitions,
summer art classes, and community workshops.

The name (g.o.d.) was deliberate. Davao has a strong arts scene that doesn't always get
the infrastructure it deserves. We tried to be part of fixing that.

## What I Ran

- Co-founded and helped define the gallery's curatorial direction
- Designed and built the web presence (Joomla initially, later migrated to WordPress)
- Created and managed social media marketing campaigns
- Organized exhibitions in collaboration with local artists
- Produced summer art classes and skill workshops open to the public

## What I Learned

Running a cultural space on a shoestring teaches you to be resourceful fast.
You learn what actually brings people through a door versus what just looks good
on a flyer. And you learn that community infrastructure — the boring, unglamorous
kind — is what makes creative scenes sustainable.
