## Overview

Since 2010 I have worked independently as a visual designer and web developer,
taking on clients across a wide range of industries: hotels, retail businesses,
non-profit organizations, government-funded programs, and cultural institutions.

This archive covers the visual design work — brand identities, print collateral,
digital assets, web layouts, social media campaigns, and more. The full portfolio
lives on Behance.

## What's In Here

- Brand identity systems (logos, style guides, collateral)
- Web design and development (WordPress, HTML/CSS)
- Print design (brochures, books, exhibition materials)
- Digital media (social campaigns, email newsletters, ads)
- Storyboarding and video content direction

## A Note on Range

The client list is deliberately varied. Working across sectors — from a film festival
to an Australian Government development program to a mountain guiding startup —
means every engagement required translating a different organizational culture
into a coherent visual language. That range is the skill.
